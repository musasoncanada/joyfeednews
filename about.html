About JoyFeed News

JoyFeed News is a tiny website that aggregates wholesome, uplifting stories from trusted RSS sources. We do lightweight keyword filtering to avoid heavy topics and bubble up the feel-good stuff.

How it works

  1. Fetch feeds via a serverless function.
  2. Filter & categorize in code (transparent and tweakable).
  3. Show recent stories on a fast, accessible front end.
