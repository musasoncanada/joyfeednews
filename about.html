About JoyFeed News

JoyFeed News curates uplifting stories from reputable sources worldwide, including BBC, Reuters, NPR, AP, and more.

We filter out heavy topics and highlight good news — kindness, science breakthroughs, conservation, clean energy, and more.

Filter by category (Community, Science, Arts, Climate, Humanity, Wildlife…) or by region (Canada, Africa, Asia, North America, Europe).

Our mission is simple: fight doomscrolling with good news 🌍✨.
